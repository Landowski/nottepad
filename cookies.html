Cookies Policy

At Online Notepad (nottepad.com), we use cookies to ensure the proper functioning of the platform, improve your experience, and display personalized ads. This policy explains how and why we use cookies and what your options are regarding them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can be used to remember your preferences, keep your session active, and display relevant ads based on your interests.

How Do We Use Cookies?

The cookies we use can be categorized as follows:

1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for Online Notepad to function properly. They allow you to log in and keep your session active while using the service. Without these cookies, some site features may not work correctly.

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies: We use cookies to understand how users interact with the site, analyzing metrics such as the number of visitors, most accessed pages, and time spent on the site. This helps us improve the user experience. For this analysis, we may use services like Google Analytics.

3. Advertising Cookies: Our site displays ads from Google AdSense and other advertising platforms. These services may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing behavior. Advertising cookies allow advertisers to provide personalized ads, making them more relevant to you.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage the use of cookies directly in your browser. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect some site functionalities.

To manage cookies in your browser, follow the links below for specific instructions:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we use third-party services that may store cookies on your device. These services include:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying personalized ads.
  • Google Analytics: For traffic and behavior analysis on the site.

These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your interactions with the site and display personalized ads based on this data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Any changes will be published on this page, and we recommend reviewing this policy regularly.
