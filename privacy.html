Privacy Policy

At Online Notepad (nottepad.com), we respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data securely and transparently. Our tool allows you to create and save notes and task lists securely in your Google Drive. Below, we detail how your data is processed.

Data Collection

When you use Online Notepad with Google Drive integration, your notes and task lists are saved securely in your own Google Drive account. All data is stored in a single JSON file within a 'Nottepad' folder created specifically in your Google Drive. We don't store any of your information on our servers - everything remains securely in your Google account, protected by Google's robust encryption and authentication systems.

Security

Your notes are stored with the highest security standards using Google Drive's infrastructure. This includes:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit
  • Google's advanced authentication protocols
  • Storage exclusively in your personal Google Drive account
  • No copies or backups stored anywhere else

Use of Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience. By continuing, you agree to our Cookie Policy. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to enhance the user interface and experience. They are not used to access your notes or personal information stored in Google Drive.

Data Sharing

Your notes remain exclusively in your Google Drive and are not shared with us or third parties. Since we don't collect or store your notes on our servers, we have no access to the content you create and cannot share it with anyone. The only data we process
