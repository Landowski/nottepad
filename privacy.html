Privacy Policy

At Online Notepad (nottepad.com), we respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data securely and transparently. Our tool allows you to create and save notes and task lists locally in your browser. Below, we detail how your data is processed.

Data Collection

When you use Online Notepad, your notes and task lists are saved locally in your browser using local storage. No personal data or notes are sent to our servers. This means your information remains private and stored only on your device.

Security

Your notes remain stored locally in your browser's storage. To ensure the security of this information, we recommend protecting access to your device and browser. Since your data never leaves your device, the security of your notes depends primarily on your device's security.

Use of Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience. By continuing, you agree to our Cookie Policy. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to enhance the user interface and experience. They are not used to access your notes or personal information.

Data Sharing

Your notes remain on your device and are not shared with us or third parties. Since we don't collect or store your notes on our servers, we have no access to the content you create and cannot share it with anyone.

Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove all notes, or you can manually delete individual notes within the application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or applicable regulations. Any changes will be posted on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about any modifications.

Contact

You can contact us via email at landowskiapps@gmail.com. Thank you for using Online Notepad and for trusting our platform to organize your notes and task lists.
